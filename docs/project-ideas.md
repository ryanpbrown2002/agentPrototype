# Class Project Ideas: Problems/Opportunities
 
**COS 598/498: Generative AI Agents -- Spring 2026 -- University of Maine**
 
1. **Community AI Steering Agent** -- AI agent that facilitates communities to collectively steer implementation and limits on use of AI agents, including facilitating community discussing, designing and co-designing desired AI integration, pushing back against undesirable integration.
 
2. **AI Use Reflection & Self-Regulation Agent** -- AI agent that helps you reflect on how you use AI, what "right relationship/use" of AI agents is for you, and helps you self-regulate to steer your AI use and non-use to be better
   - helps you set your boundaries
   - helps you experiment with how you use AI
   - helps by proposing alternative things that are not just about AI such as:
     - recommend to do body-doubling to help with motivation/focus
     - helps you touch grass when you are using AI too much
 
3. **AI Agent Use Trainer/Tutor** -- teaches a person how to use AI agents, what to avoid, gives examples, does interactive practice simulating/doing AI agent use then facilitating feedback and reflection. Teaches critical and ethical use of AI.
 
4. **Neurodiversity Support Agent** -- AI agent that helps people with neurodiversity magnify benefits of their neurodiversity and navigate social systems/practices that may not have been designed to support them well.
 
5. **Learning Community Connector Agent** -- AI agent that connects people around common interests for doing learning outside of classes together. Helps people plan that learning/projects, collaborate, have everyone in the group get good things out of that learning and collaboration.
 
6. **AI Agent Authoring & Recommender/Search Tool** -- for finding existing agents (commercial or open-source) and evaluating them to make recommendations on what to use and how to use it (and not use it). Stretch goal: helping users edit/refine an AI agent as they use it i.e. changing its code/prompts, modifying data sources, etc.
 
7. **AI Capabilities Mental Model Agent** -- An AI agent that helps people form better mental models of what AI agent systems are actually capable of, what is hype versus reality, how trained person + AI can do more than an AI system, teaching and showing that AI "capabilities" are always socially situated and often involve and require users more than one might think or be hyped about.
 
8. **Student-Centered Education Redesign Agent** -- AI agents that work backwards from the actual goals of students, to improve education by, for example, finding new and alternative ways to demonstrate competency that align with their career goals and meaning and communities. The AI agent would help students/faculty redesign course assignments (or even entire courses), such as generating personalized learning materials and assignments for small groups of students with similar goals/interests.
 
9. **Organizing for Good Causes Agent** -- AI agent that facilitates organizing for good causes, such as joining a community of practice around unionizing/organizing for worker's rights and interests as AI agents enter the workplace (in desirable and undesirable ways).
 
10. **Mindful Consumption Agent** -- An AI agent that facilitates a person or community in reducing their wants for things they do not actually need, as a counter to messages from advertising, wasteful consumerism, etc. Encourages human flourishing by, for example, encouraging self-care, empathy, etc.
 
11. **Ethical Careers Agent** -- An ethical and meaningful careers AI agent that facilitates people in searching for jobs, applying for jobs, joining communities of practice, connecting with others for side-projects (or even starting new companies or non-profits) during job search.
